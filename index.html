About Me

Howdy howdy! Y’all can call me Artzy. My birthday is 3/2/04 and I’m an artist! I’m self taught and I’ve been doing art since I was little, but I only started putting more effort into my art back in 2019. I mostly do digital art, but I do a lot of other stuff in my spare time, like painting, sculpting, sewing, coding, etc. Back in 2021 I was diagnosed with Generalized Tonic-clonic Epilepsy after I had multiple seizures. I’ve been on medication since then and it helps a lot, but I’m still sensitive to stress, flashing lights and colors, and many other things. So if I'm not active on social media that often it’s because I’m taking care of my health.

As of now, I'm only making money from Commissions and Streaming on Twitch, so simply watching the ads on my live streams is super duper helpful and appreciated and really helps to support me.

You can help support me through my Twitch, my Kofi or Commissions! Otherwise, just sharing my stuff helps a bunch!

You can also tip me here!
